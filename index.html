Fabo Bio
I was born in Brazil and moved to Peru since 5 years old. My native language is portuguese, then I learned spanish and have used that much of my life. I love the Amazon river and the forest but I am also fascinated by the Matrix. I met my first computer in 2000, a Dell laptop brought by my father, an engineer at the Central Reserve Bank of Peru. I was definitely surprised by the possibilities of this toy and what I learned from it, so I kept the vice. At 18, I entered the National Engineering University [UNI] to study architecture, I had a good time proposing new models of education, creating forums, a magazine and inviting Richard Stallman to the campus with some friends. I believe in open source, free software and liberation of knowledge to catalyze our human evolutionary process, whatever it is we call "evolution" or "humanity". What stage will we be in? In 2014 I discovered Bitcoin, but in fact I was busy creating and failing an application about art contests, and it was not until December 2018 that I began to participate in algorithmic trading with machine learning, I came to give lecture on that at the Pontifical Catholic University of Peru [PUCP]. Now I am dedicated to research in blockchain, 3D modeling and animation. Do you want to talk about it?
